# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Board Decision Owner Continuity Scorecard is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- convert handoff sprawl, owner drift, and continuity breaks into explicit board-visible continuity tradeoffs
- expose where locking a named owner-of-record can recover decision speed before another lane is funded
- tie continuity coverage and board confidence to named operating surfaces instead of abstract ownership complaints
- convert scattered continuity bottlenecks into one reusable intervention packet for boards and investors
